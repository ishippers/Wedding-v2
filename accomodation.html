Getting to Charleston

Flying

If you’re planning on flying directly, the local airport is Charleston (CHS). Do not get Charleston, South Carolina confused with Charleston, West Virginia ― you’ll end up a long way off! Most flights into Charleston are via Atlanta or JFK. We normally fly with Delta or US Airways but you can always do a quick search on Sky Scanner to find the best deal.

Driving

Charleston is accessible by Highway 17 and Interstate 26.

Where to Stay

When looking for places to stay in Charleston, please be aware that “Charleston” can refer to the wider metropolitan area, not just the historic downtown area.

In fact everything in the map below can be considered “Charleston.”

We would suggest staying in either downtown Charleston (labeled Charleston above) or on Folly Beach.


Where ever you decide to stay hiring a car will be a necessity there is lots to do and see all over Charleston and public transport is sparse (to be honest, as a South Carolina native, Megan has never used public transport in Charleston!)

On the day of the wedding , you will not need to arrange any transportation to the venue as we will arrange for buses to shuttle you to and from Magnolia Plantation.
