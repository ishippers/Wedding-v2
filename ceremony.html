Ceremony and Reception

Both the Ceremony and Reception will take place at the Carriage House, Magnolia Plantation, 3550 Ashley River Road, Charleston, SC 29414. The ceremony will start promptly at 5.30pm and will be followed by a cocktail hour, dinner, and dancing until 11pm. Dress code is formal, the ceremony will be on the lawn so please keep this in mind when choosing your footwear!

How to Get There

We will be providing transportation for those who do not wish to drive. This will be leaving from Folly Beach at 4.15pm and will bring you back after the reception. If you would like to drive Magnolia is located on Highway 61 and is approximately 10 miles from downtown Charleston.

Menu

Dinner is inspired by Low Country classics, with some British and Australian favourites during cocktail hour.

  • Caesar salad
  • Charleston chicken with cheddar and garlic mashed potatoes and southern style green beans
  • Cakes and s'mores
