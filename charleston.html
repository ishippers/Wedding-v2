More about the city

For those of you who aren’t familiar with Charleston, it is the oldest city in South Carolina and is known for well-preserved architecture.


An iconic part of Charleston’s skyline is the Arthur Ravenell, Jr. bridge, which has a public footpath and is a great way to get a bird’s eye view of the city and the port.
